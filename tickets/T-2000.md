# T-2000: Calendar + Siler Mortality + Pregnancy System

**Phase**: 2-A1
**Priority**: High
**Status**: In Progress

## Summary
Implement proper Gregorian calendar, Siler(1979) bathtub mortality model,
and Gaussian gestation pregnancy system with preterm birth mechanics.

## Deliverables
1. GameCalendar: 365-day Gregorian calendar with leap years, seasons
2. MortalitySystem: Siler 3-component mortality (μ = a₁e^{-b₁x} + a₂ + a₃e^{b₃x})
3. PregnancySystem: Gaussian gestation (μ=280d, σ=10d), preterm, maternal complications
4. 7 age stages: infant/toddler/child/teen/adult/elder/ancient
5. Entity frailty field (N(1.0, 0.15), clamped [0.5, 2.0])
6. HUD date: "Y3 7월 15일 14:00 (여름)"
7. Annual demography log
8. docs/RESEARCH_REFERENCES.md with academic citations
